## Detailed Description

### Boost Your Conversion Rate with AI-Powered Insights

ConversionAI analyzes your Shopify store and generates specific, actionable recommendations to increase conversions and revenue. Powered by advanced AI, it identifies optimization opportunities across your entire customer journey.

**What You Get:**

- **12+ Prioritized Recommendations** - Each analysis generates actionable CRO recommendations ranked by impact and effort
- **ROI Estimates** - See the potential monthly revenue increase for each recommendation
- **Implementation Code** - Get ready-to-use code snippets for technical changes
- **Step-by-Step Guidance** - Clear implementation steps for every recommendation
- **Visual Store Analysis** - AI analyzes your homepage and product pages visually

**Areas Analyzed:**

- Hero section & above-the-fold content
- Product pages & add-to-cart experience
- Cart & checkout optimization
- Mobile responsiveness
- Trust signals & social proof
- Urgency & scarcity elements
- Navigation & site speed

**How It Works:**

1. Install the app and connect your store
2. Click "Run Analysis" - our AI analyzes your store data and captures screenshots
3. Receive 12+ prioritized recommendations within 2-3 minutes
4. Implement changes and track your improvements

**Pricing:**

- **Free Plan**: 1 analysis per month
- **Pro Plan ($29/mo)**: Unlimited analyses, weekly auto-refresh, priority support
- **Enterprise ($99/mo)**: API access, custom integrations, dedicated support

**Why Merchants Love ConversionAI:**

- Business-focused recommendations (revenue & conversion %, not technical jargon)
- Quick wins identified first (low effort, high impact)
- Works with any Shopify theme
- No coding required for most recommendations
- Results you can measure

**Built by ApexMind AI Labs** - We specialize in AI-powered e-commerce optimization tools.
